How ProContracts™ Works

The world's first dual-dimension professional marketplace · Patent Pending 2026

Core Innovation: Every professional platform categorizes people by job title only. ProContracts™ is the first to match professionals across two dimensions: their Profession (what they do) and their Industry Experience (where they have done it).

For Companies & Clients

Use our dual filter system to find professionals who match both what you need and who understand your industry context. A startup in the IT sector searching for financial help can specifically find Finance professionals who have worked inside IT companies — not generic accountants.

  • Select the Profession you need (Finance, Legal, IT, Design, Marketing, HR)
  • Select the Industry your business operates in
  • Browse ranked, verified professionals who match both
  • View full profiles and request a contract directly

For Professionals

Build a dual-dimension profile that shows not just what you do — but where you've done it. This makes you discoverable to exactly the right companies.

  • Select your core Profession
  • Select all industries where you have real experience
  • Complete verification via Aadhaar-based KYC
  • Get ranked based on verified hires and client ratings
  • Get discovered by companies who need your exact combination

The Ranking System

Professionals are ranked within their profession-industry combination based on total verified hires, client ratings, peer endorsements, and years of experience. Rankings are transparent, updated monthly, and shown publicly on every profile.

Verification

All professionals go through a multi-step verification process including identity verification, credential checks, and video KYC. Verified professionals display a green ✓ badge on their profile.

Pricing

Browsing professionals is completely free. Companies pay only when they successfully hire through the platform. Professionals pay a small monthly subscription to maintain a verified, ranked profile.
